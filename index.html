Our sources

Every sector checks these, in order, moving to the next only when the previous one has nothing for that category:

  1. That sector's own dedicated trade press — 2 feeds per sector (shown at the top of each card below, e.g. TechCrunch + Ars Technica for Technology).
  2. A shared pool of 11 general finance/policy sources — Chatham House, Foreign Affairs (CFR), AEI, NPR Business, NYT Business, WSJ Business, Schwab's WashingtonWise podcast, Fox Business Markets, Farnam Street, Utility Dive, and HousingWire.
  3. Marketaux — a financial news aggregator pulling from thousands of publishers, including Yahoo Finance, Seeking Alpha, Motley Fool, Reuters, and many more, with articles tagged by which companies they mention.
  4. GNews — a broad general news search API, used as a final fallback if all of the above come up empty.

That's 13+ named sources plus two large aggregators spanning thousands of publishers between them — cached once per day so it stays fast and light on API quotas.
